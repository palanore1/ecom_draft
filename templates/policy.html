Privacy Policy - Ecom Draft

Last updated: March 08, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Customer Calls ("we," "us," or "our"). This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your personal information when you use our web application ("EcomDraft"). The EcomDraft App automates customer order confirmations via phone calls and integrates with Shopify for order management. By using the EcomDraft App, you agree to the practices described herein. If you do not agree, please do not use the EcomDraft App.

2. Information We Collect

We collect various types of information to provide and improve our services:

2.1 Personal Information

  • User Account Data: Name, email address, and profile picture collected via Google OAuth during login. We also request read-only access to your Google Spreadsheets to enable importing customer data.
  • Contact Information: Phone numbers of customers, collected from Google Sheets you provide.
  • Customer Data: Names, addresses, and order details (e.g., order value, order number) from Google Sheets.

2.2 Shopify Integration Data

  • Store Credentials: Store name, Shopify Shop URL, and Shopify Access Token, voluntarily provided by you to enable order updates.
  • Order Information: Order IDs and statuses updated via Shopify API based on customer responses.

2.3 Usage Data

  • App Interactions: Call statuses (e.g., Confirmed, Declined), stored temporarily in Redis.
  • Technical Data: IP addresses, browser type, and device information logged during App usage.

2.4 How Information Is Collected

We collect information directly from you (e.g., via login or settings input), from Google Sheets you upload, and automatically through App usage and third-party integrations.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • Service Delivery: Authenticate users via Google OAuth, make automated calls via Twilio, and update Shopify orders based on customer responses.
  • Data Management: Store call statuses in Redis and customer data in our database for processing.
  • Improvement: Analyze usage patterns to enhance EcomDraft App functionality.
  • Communication: Send service-related notifications (e.g., call confirmations via Shopify).
  • Legal Compliance: Meet regulatory requirements or respond to legal requests.

4. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share it as follows:

4.1 Third-Party Service Providers

  • Google: For OAuth authentication.
  • Twilio: For automated phone calls (receives phone numbers and call statuses).
  • Shopify: For order updates (receives order IDs and statuses).
  • Redis: For temporary data storage (hosted via our Redis provider).

4.2 Legal Obligations

We may disclose information if required by law, court order, or to protect our rights, property, or safety.

4.3 Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale, your information may be transferred as part of our assets.

5. Data Storage and Security

We store your data in a secure SQL database and Redis instance. Personal information is retained as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. Call statuses in Redis expire after 24 hours (or 5 minutes for temporary keys). We implement measures like encryption, access controls, and secure servers to protect your data, but no system is 100% secure.

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data.
  • Correction: Update inaccurate information.
  • Deletion: Request removal of your data (subject to legal obligations).
  • Opt-Out: Limit certain data uses (e.g., stop using the EcomDraft App to halt processing).
  • Portability: Receive your data in a structured format.
